# AI Task Packet

Give this to the AI coding tool you use.

## Keep in mind

Help the user build this product step by step. If something is unclear or risky, ask first. Do not make real-world decisions for the user.

## Rules

- Summarize the goal first.
- Ask when important details are unclear.
- Do one small task at a time.
- Do not expand first-version scope.
- Ask before actions that affect users, production data, payments, review, or launch.
- Leave proof after completion.
