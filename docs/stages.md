# Jumao Gates

## 0. Idea gate

Say clearly: who it is for, what it solves, and why it should exist now.

## 1. First-version scope gate

Write what is in and out. Do not let AI expand the product by itself.

## 2. Screen-state gate

For each screen, define loading, empty, error, permission-denied, and success states.

## 3. Data-safety gate

For each data item, define source, purpose, storage, and deletion.

## 4. Implementation gate

Break work into small tasks and let AI change only one clear area at a time.

## 5. Verification gate

Keep at least one proof type: tests, screenshots, logs, or human review.

## 6. Release gate

Launch, review submission, production deployment, payment changes, or anything affecting real users needs human confirmation.
